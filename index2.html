About Us

FXG IT Solutions Limited is a software development company that is established to provide bespoke solutions for both private and public sectors. Our services range from consultancy service to delivering software (web and mobile) applications to her clients and this is aligned towards providing solutions for small, medium and large-scale business seamlessly to project requirements and business needs across the world.

Our company is built around strong and technical expertise by leveraging on hands-on experience to provide value-added business applications.
